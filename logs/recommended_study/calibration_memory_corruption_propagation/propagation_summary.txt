=============================

Corrupted coolant-control target calibration campaign
-----------------------------------------------------
Campaign ID: calibration_memory_corruption
Duration: 120.0 s

Research framing: Strong computation/memory demo: corrupted calibration shifts controller behavior before thermal and safety consequences become visible.

Propagation Chain
-----------------
1. Hardware-Origin Fault
   Story: Corrupted calibration register or nonvolatile memory bit upset affecting the cooling-control target.
   Evidence: calibration memory corruption (permanent) at 52.0 s during highway load
   Detail: permanent fault active, parameter=16.000
2. ECU Manifestation
   Story: The ECU continues with a delayed cooling request even though measured temperatures are correct, because the internal target is shifted.
   Evidence: Cooling request remains delayed despite rising temperature at 60.7 s during urban traffic
   Detail: Coolant reached 95.11 C while pump command was 0.265 and fan command was 0.000.
3. Diagnostic Effect
   Story: Thermal and cooling-performance evidence appears later than in direct actuation faults because the error begins inside control computation.
   Evidence: Primary DTC: cooling performance low at 91.5 s during hot idle
   Detail: Primary diagnostic evidence first appears as cooling performance low.
4. Safe-State / System Effect
   Story: Coolant temperature rises higher than nominal and protection eventually escalates, illustrating delayed cross-layer propagation.
   Evidence: Thermal warning threshold crossed at 91.5 s during hot idle
   Detail: Coolant temperature crossed the warning threshold.
